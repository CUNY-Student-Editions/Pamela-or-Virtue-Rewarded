Loo: or lanterloo, a card-game in which ‘Pam’, the knave of clubs, is the highest trump (though SR does not appear to be playing on this detail). Cf. John Crowne, Sir Courtly Nice (1685), Act III: ‘Thou art the only Court Card Women love to play with; the very Pam at Lantereloo, the Knave that picks up all.’
