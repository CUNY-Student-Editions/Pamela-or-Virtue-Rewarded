a Justice of Peace: county magistrate (usually a leading landowner), empowered with summary jurisdiction in minor cases, and responsible for committing accused felons to trial for more serious crimes. Pamela fears the provisions of a 1713 Act (12 Anne, c. 7), aimed specifically at domestic servants, which removed from benefit of clergy (i.e. made punishable by death) theft from a dwelling-house of goods valued above forty shillings.
