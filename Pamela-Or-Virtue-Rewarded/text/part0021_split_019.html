Grashopper in the Fable … in my Lady’s Books: no. 164 in SR’s edition of …sop’s Fables (1739), reprinted above, Appendix 2. Presumably the library includes Sir Roger L’Estrange’s lavish Fables, of …sop and Other Eminent Mythologists (1692), on which the wording of SR’s version is based (though Pamela’s ‘very words’ here, in their minor variants, are those of the 1739 adaptation).
