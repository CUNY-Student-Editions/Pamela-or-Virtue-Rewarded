speaking Picture: i.e. actress, dissembler. The phrase derives from Sidney’s account, in the Defence of Poetry, of the Aristotelian concept of mimesis, and recurs in eighteenth-century discussions of visual or dramatic illusion (e.g. John Gay, ‘Epistle to Pulteney’ (1720), 1. 218; Aaron Hill, The Art of Acting (1746), 1. 265).
