When the noble Family … Duchess of York: referring to the scandalous marriage in 1660 of the Duke of York (afterwards James II) to Anne, daughter of Edward Hyde (who was ennobled a few weeks later and created Earl of Clarendon the following year). See above, p. 269 and n.; also C. D. Williams, ‘Pamela and the Case of the Slandered Duchess’, SEL 29(1989), 515–33.
