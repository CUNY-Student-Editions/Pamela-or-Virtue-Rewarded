Sack-whey: medicinal drink, made with fortified wine from Spain or the Canaries, and used as a diaphoretic. Mr B. is following the cold cure recommended by SR’s friend and physician George Cheyne (1671–1743): ‘Lye much a-bed; drink plentifully of small, warm Sack-Whey’ (Gentleman’s Magazine, 6 (Oct. 1736), 619).
