But I had no Arms to quarter … quarter’d for mine: as a low-born wife, Pamela has no hereditary arms to link with her husband’s in the usual manner, i.e. in alternate quarters on a heraldic shield. The Olive-branch would represent offspring (after Psalms 128: 3): cf. ‘My most humble duty to my lord, and best wishes to the olive branches’ (Letters of George Cheyne to the Countess of Huntingdon, ed. C. F. Mullett (1940), 45 (23 July 1735)).
